About Us

"The company"

"The company" was founded in 1868 with a clear idea of making the purchasing of yachts and other luxury boats more accessable for everyday people. The founders Olle Gordon, John Fredrik Maximilian Schürer von Waldheim and Telly Lange met one day by Lady Fate's calling, the three musketeers, Olle Gordon, John Fredrik Maximilian Schürer von Waldheim, and Telly Lange, each brought their unique skills and passions to the table. Olle was a seasoned sailor with a deep love for the sea, John was a visionary entrepreneur with a knack for business, and Telly was a charismatic salesman with a gift for connecting with people from all walks of life. Driven by their shared vision of making luxury yachts more accessible, they pooled their resources and expertise to establish their company in 1868. Their mission was ambitious yet simple: to democratize the world of yachting and bring the joy of sailing to as many people as possible. Despite facing numerous challenges and skeptics along the way, the trio persevered, fueled by their unwavering determination and passion for their cause. They traveled the world, forging connections with shipbuilders, designers, and enthusiasts, and gradually built a reputation for themselves as pioneers in the industry. Over the years, their company grew and evolved, adapting to changing times and tastes while staying true to its founding principles. From humble beginnings, they expanded their offerings to include a wide range of luxury boats, catering to a diverse clientele from around the globe. Today, the legacy of Olle, John, and Telly lives on, inspiring a new generation of sailors and enthusiasts to pursue their dreams of adventure on the open seas. And as their company continues to thrive, it remains a testament to the power of passion, perseverance, and the enduring spirit of exploration.

"Our Principles"

For over 150 years, "The company" has specialized in the selling and distribution of high-end seafaring vehicles. We focus on building personal relationships with our customers so we can offer the best service and prices. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a sterling reputation in the industry, making us the go-to choice for discerning sailors and yacht enthusiasts worldwide. From sleek, modern yachts to classic, timeless vessels, we curate a diverse portfolio to cater to every taste and preference. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing personalized guidance and support throughout the entire purchasing process, ensuring that each client finds the perfect vessel to suit their needs. But our dedication doesn't stop at the point of sale. We believe in fostering long-term partnerships with our customers, offering ongoing maintenance and support services to keep their seafaring dreams alive for years to come. Whether it's routine maintenance, upgrades, or emergency repairs, our skilled technicians are always on hand to provide prompt and reliable assistance. At "The company," we understand that owning a luxury yacht is more than just a purchase—it's a lifestyle. That's why we strive to exceed expectations at every turn, delivering unparalleled quality, service, and value to our valued clientele. With us, the journey doesn't end when you set sail—it's just the beginning of a lifelong adventure on the open seas.
