About Us

Our History

Boats Inc. was founded in 1868 with a clear idea of making the purchasing of yachts and other luxury boats more accessable for everyday people. The founders Olle Gordon, John Fredrik Maximilian Schürer von Waldheim and Telly Lange met one day by Lady Fate's calling, the three musketeers, Olle Gordon, John Fredrik Maximilian Schürer von Waldheim and Telly Lange, each brought their unique skills and passions to the table. Olle was a seasoned sailor with a deep love for the sea, John was a visionary entrepreneur with a knack for business, and Telly was a charismatic salesman with a gift for connecting with people from all walks of life. Driven by their shared vision of making luxury yachts more accessible, they pooled their resources and expertise to establish their company in 1868. Their mission was ambitious yet simple: to democratize the world of yachting and bring the joy of sailing to as many people as possible. Despite facing numerous challenges and skeptics along the way, the trio persevered, fueled by their unwavering determination and passion for their cause. They traveled the world, forging connections with shipbuilders, designers, and enthusiasts, and gradually built a reputation for themselves as pioneers in the industry. Over the years, their company grew and evolved, adapting to changing times and tastes while staying true to its founding principles. From humble beginnings, they expanded their offerings to include a wide range of luxury boats, catering to a diverse clientele from around the globe. Today, the legacy of Olle, John, and Telly lives on, inspiring a new generation of sailors and enthusiasts to pursue their dreams of adventure on the open seas. And as their company continues to thrive, it remains a testament to the power of passion, perseverance, and the enduring spirit of exploration.

Our Journey

For over a century and a half, Boats Inc. has been dedicated to the selling and distribution of premier seafaring vessels. Our cornerstone is cultivating personal connections with our clientele, ensuring optimal service and pricing. Renowned for our unwavering commitment to excellence and client satisfaction, we've garnered a sterling reputation worldwide, becoming the preferred choice for discerning sailors and yacht aficionados alike. From cutting-edge, contemporary yachts to timeless classics, our collection caters to diverse tastes. Our seasoned professionals offer tailored guidance through the entire purchasing journey, ensuring each client finds their ideal vessel. Yet, our dedication extends beyond the sale. We prioritize long-term relationships, providing ongoing maintenance and support to uphold seafaring dreams for generations. Whether routine upkeep, enhancements, or urgent repairs, our adept technicians deliver swift, dependable assistance. At Boats Inc. we recognize that yacht ownership transcends mere acquisition—it embodies a lifestyle.
